My Journey Through Nature

Hi, I'm Vivian, a natural explorer, deeply curious about the world. My journey has taken me from wild landscapes to lines of code. I began learning software development to better understand how things work behind the screen, but I always return to nature to reset and recharge.

This blog is my space to share the hikes that have moved me, trails where the stillness of the forest, the texture of volcanic rock, or the sound of waves has helped me reconnect with myself.

I believe hiking is more than exercise; it's meditation, perspective, and joy. Whether you’re a solo traveler, a weekend wanderer, or someone who’s just curious, I hope you’ll find a trail here that speaks to you.
