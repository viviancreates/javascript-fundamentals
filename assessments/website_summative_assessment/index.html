Featured Hikes

Azores, Portugal

Lush volcanic landscapes, crater lakes, and ocean views make São Miguel’s hiking trails unforgettable.

Snoqualmie Pass, Washington

Just an hour from Seattle, this trail offers forested paths, alpine lakes, and mountain views.

Oregon Coast

Follow forested headlands and open beaches with endless Pacific views.

Petra, Jordan

Hike to the ancient city of Petra via the Dana Reserve, Wadi Feynan, and desert canyons carved into red sandstone.

Cocora Valley, Colombia

Trek through lush green hills, towering wax palms, and authentic coffee farms in Cocora Valley.

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

This short, steep trail on Dois Irmãos starts in Vidigal and ends with panoramic views over Rio’s beaches, favelas, and skyline
